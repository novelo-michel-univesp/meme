The Story

IT professionals are often celebrated as problem solvers in the digital realm. However, their day-to-day experiences are far more diverse—and occasionally absurd. From fixing coffee machines to setting up printers on the moon (or so it feels), the life of an IT technician is never boring. TIAnyThing was born out of these moments, showcasing the versatility, humor, and sometimes frustration of those in the tech world.

Funny Stories

The Printer Whisperer

A frantic office worker called IT because the printer wouldn't turn on. The "problem"? It wasn't plugged in.

Coffee Machine IT

"It's not making coffee!" After thorough troubleshooting, the IT guy discovered the coffee pod was inserted upside down.

The "Self-Fixing" Monitor

A frustrated user called IT to report that their monitor wasn’t turning on. The technician checked the setup and found the monitor wasn’t plugged in. The user responded: “I thought monitors were wireless these days!”.

Mouse Without a Tail

An employee complained their mouse wasn’t working. Upon inspection, the IT tech realized the "mouse" was a wireless one, but the user had been trying to connect a computer mouse to a USB cable meant for a camera. The user insisted, “It fits, doesn’t it? It should work!”

The Overheating Computer

A user reported that their PC kept shutting down unexpectedly. The technician discovered the computer was placed under a table with a heater directly beside it. The user’s reasoning? “I thought it would keep it warm and make it work faster!”

The “Waterproof” Laptop

A manager brought in a soaked laptop, claiming, “I thought it could survive a coffee spill because it’s ‘liquid-cooled.’” The technician had to explain the difference between internal cooling systems and external liquids
